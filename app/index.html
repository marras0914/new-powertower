About
Pacific Union Financial
In 2004 we started out with a mission to serve the underserved markets in government lending. Since it’s inception, Pacific Union has built a business serving lenders, brokers and borrowers based on relationships of trust, integrity and respect. We are not a pass-through – we retain servicing on our loans and take care of our borrowers. We don’t solicit borrowers for other products – we protect your lending relationships with your customers.
